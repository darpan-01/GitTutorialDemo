Krishna

Which clan does Krishna belong to?

Krishna was born into the Yadava clan. He was the son of Vasudeva and Devaki, who was the sister of Kamsa, the wicked king of Mathura.

Who is Krishna's wife?

Krishna married Princess Rukmini and took other wives as well.

Who raised Krishna?

Krishna was the son of Vasudeva and Devaki but, when his maternal uncle Kamsa, the wicked king of Mathura, tried to kill him, he was smuggled across the Yamuna River to Gokula and raised by the leader of the cowherds, Nanda and his wife Yashoda.

How is Krishna depicted?

The most common representation of Krishna shows him playing the flute and surrounded by adoring gopis, the wives and daughters of the cowherds. As a child, Krishna is depicted crawling on his hands and knees or dancing with joy, a ball of butter held in his hands.

Krishna is an avatar of which Hindu deity?

Krishna, one of the most widely revered and most popular of all Indian divinities, is worshipped as the eighth incarnation (avatar, or avatara) of the Hindu god Vishnu and also as a supreme god in his own right.
